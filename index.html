A Simple Letter Can Change Everything

In a world of fleeting digital pings, the simple act of receiving a personal letter can feel like magic. It's a tangible piece of kindness, a reminder that someone, somewhere, is thinking of you.

At Yours Truly, we're dedicated to bottling that feeling of warmth and connection and sending it to those who need it most. This is a mission of the heart, powered by people like you. If you believe in the power of a simple letter, we invite you to join us.

Turning Words into Warmth

Here’s how we deliver a little bit of happiness, step by step.

Step 1: You Nominate

You tell us about someone who needs a little cheer through a private, simple form.

Step 2: We Write

We read their story and write a personal, uplifting letter just for them.

Step 3: We Perfect It

We make sure every letter is positive and beautiful before printing it on lovely stationery.

Step 4: We Deliver

The letter is mailed, arriving as a wonderful, tangible surprise in their mailbox.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out.

Yours Truly is a social impact project dedicated to combating loneliness and spreading joy. We send free, personal, and heartfelt letters to people who need encouragement, a reminder that they are valued, and a tangible piece of kindness in their mailbox.

You can nominate anyone who you feel would benefit from a warm, encouraging letter. This includes elderly individuals, patients in hospitals, those facing mental health challenges, unsung community heroes, or simply anyone going through a tough time who needs to know someone cares.

Yes, absolutely. There is no cost to nominate someone or for the recipient to receive a letter. Our mission is funded by us and by donations from generous supporters who believe in the power of connection.

We require the nominee's name and full mailing address and some essential details to send the letter. This information is only used for facilitating the operations.

Protecting privacy is our top priority. The nominee's personal information is kept strictly confidential and is only accessible to our core team. We never share or sell data. All nominations are handled with the utmost care and discretion. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

Our letters are crafted by a small, dedicated team of vetted designers who are passionate about our mission. Each writer is trained to compose graphics design, ensuring every letter is a source of genuine comfort and joy.

Yes. While we ask for your name, email and contact number on the nomination form (so we can contact you if there's an issue with the address), your identity will not be revealed to the letter recipient, if anonymity is opted. But, in case of legal issues we might be asked to provide details to concerned authority.

Every single donation goes directly towards funding our mission. Contributions cover the costs of high-quality stationery, envelopes, postage stamps, and printing. This allows us to keep the entire service free for both nominators and recipients.
