Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Yours Truly ("we," "our," or "us") is deeply committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal information we handle. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard information in connection with our social impact initiative and website (the "Service").

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to fulfill our mission of sending letters.

2.1. Information Provided to Us

  • Nomination Data: When a "Nominator" submits a nomination, we collect the Nominator's name and contact information (email, phone number). We also collect personal information about the "Nominee," which includes their name, full mailing address, and the personal story provided by the Nominator.
  • Volunteer Data: When you apply to volunteer, we collect your name, contact information, and details about your skills and interests.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message, and any other information you may choose to provide.

2.2. A Special Note on Nominee Data & Consent

We do not collect information directly from Nominees. This information is provided by Nominators. Our Terms of Use strictly require that all Nominators obtain explicit and informed consent from the Nominee before sharing their personal information with us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Operate Our Service: To review nominations, personalize and craft letters, and mail them to Nominees.
  • To Communicate: To contact Nominators if we need clarification about a nomination and to communicate with our volunteers about their roles and our activities.
  • For Administrative Purposes: To improve our service, maintain records, and ensure the security of our website.
  • To Comply with Law: To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

4. How and Why We Share Your Information

We are dedicated to maintaining the confidentiality of the stories shared with us.

  • With Our Internal Team and Vetted Volunteers: Nominee information is shared on a strict, need-to-know basis with our core team and vetted volunteers who are directly involved in the letter-writing process. All team members and volunteers are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services to operate. These include Google Forms for collecting nomination data and postal/courier services for delivering letters. These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to protect it.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against interception.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Nomination data for which a letter has been sent is retained for a limited period for record-keeping. Unsuccessful or rejected nomination data is securely deleted in a timely manner.

7. Your Privacy Rights

  • For Nominators and Volunteers: You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • For Nominees: If you have received a letter from us, you have the right to know what information we hold about you and to request its deletion. If you believe your information was shared without your consent, please contact us immediately.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. Nominators must not submit information for any individual under 18 without the verifiable consent of their parent or legal guardian.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the methods provided on our website.
